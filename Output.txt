=== Airport Sunset Scene ===
Graphics Algorithms Used:
1. DDA Line Algorithm - Runway center markings
2. Bresenham Line Algorithm - Runway edge markings
3. Midpoint Circle Algorithm - Sun, windows, lights

Transformations:
- Translation: Sun descending, airplane moving
- Scaling: Sun size changing, brightness fading

Animation loops continuously every 6 seconds!

=== CONTROLS ===
Press 'F' key to make the airplane fly higher!
Press 'D' key to make the airplane fly lower!
Press 'ESC' to exit.
